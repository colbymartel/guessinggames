Meet the Creator

Colby Martel — Creator of Guess The Singer & Guessing Games

Hi, I’m Colby!

I’m a creator from New Hampshire who loves games, music, and meaningful connection. I started Guess The Singer in 2023 as a fun way to combine my love for music and interactive challenges.

Since then, the project has grown into a community of players, fans, and college campuses who love to compete, laugh, and give back — all through games.

I believe guessing games can do more than entertain. That’s why some of our games help support scholarships, Feeding America, and even wildlife conservation.

Thanks for being here — I hope you play, share, and join me on the journey.
